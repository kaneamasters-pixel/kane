Day 1

Before you begin

Set yourself up properly. This meditation works best when your environment works with you.

1
Find your space. Sit comfortably or lie down somewhere you won't be disturbed for the next 10-15 minutes.
2
Silence everything. Phone on silent. Notifications off. This time is yours.
3
Put on binaural beats. Open Spotify or YouTube and search "theta binaural beats" or "528hz meditation music". Play it quietly in the background — this deepens the experience.
4
Close your eyes briefly. Take three deep breaths before pressing Start. Let your shoulders drop. Let your body settle.
5
Commit to the full session. Don't skip forward. Let each phase land before you move to the next.
